About Us

The firm is located in the Americas Tower on Allen Parkway near downtown Houston, where Mr. Allen has practiced law since 2007, helping corporate clients, as well as individuals, in litigation and trial.

Licenses Held

Mr. Allen is licensed to practice law in the State of Texas, as well as the United States District Court for the Southern District of Texas, the USDC for the Western District of Texas, the USDC for the Northern District of Texas, and the United States Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit.

Legal Practice Areas

Commercial Litigation

Allen & Associates is experienced in representing businesses and individuals in a wide variety of commercial litigation matters. If you or your business is facing a lawsuit, call us today to discuss your options. We have a long track record of business litigation success, and we intend to keep it that way.

If you have to file an insurance claim for any reason, chances are you already have suffered enough. We work to help businesses, individuals and families make sure they don't suffer again at the hands of their insurance company.





Franchise Law
